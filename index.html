About Me

My name is Sai, and I am a Unity 3D Developer currently living in London, UK. I am a charismatic and ambitious C# programmer, who has just graduated with a First Class in BSc Games Design and Development from the University of Greenwich.

Following the completion of my degree, I am currently working for Arup; a design, architecture, and engineering consultancy firm as a Assistant Programmer and Unity Developer. When I am not making games in my spare time, I spend the majority of it playing other people's games, with a selection of my favourites being RuneScape, Dark Souls, The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim and Final Fantasy.
